How to save as PDF

Click "Save as PDF" — in the print dialog change Destination to "Save as PDF". For wide sheets, set orientation to Landscape in print settings.

About Excel to PDF

Your spreadsheet is read in your browser using SheetJS and displayed as a formatted HTML table. No data is uploaded anywhere. Multiple sheets are supported — switch between them using the tabs above the preview.

How to Use the Excel to PDF Converter

1
Upload your spreadsheet
Click upload and select your .xlsx, .xls, or .csv file. All sheets are loaded and shown as tabs.
2
Switch between sheets
Use the sheet tabs above the preview to check each sheet before converting.
3
Save as PDF
Click Save as PDF, choose "Save as PDF" in the print dialog, set orientation to Landscape for wide sheets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Excel formulas preserved in the PDF?+
The PDF shows calculated values not formulas, which is usually what you want when sharing data with others.
What should I do for wide spreadsheets?+
In the print dialog set the orientation to Landscape and scale to Fit to Page for best results with wide sheets.
Are multiple sheets included in the PDF?+
Currently one sheet is printed at a time. Switch to the sheet you want using the tabs then click Save as PDF.
Is my spreadsheet data sent to a server?+
No. SheetJS reads and renders your file entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
