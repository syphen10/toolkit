About ToolKit

ToolKit is a collection of free, browser-based utility tools built for everyone. Whether you need to compress an image, format some JSON, generate a password, or convert a PDF — it's all here, free, with no sign up required.

Why we built this. Most online tools are bloated, slow, require accounts, or try to upsell you. We wanted something different — fast, clean, and actually free.

Privacy first. Every tool processes your files locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. Your data stays on your device.

Have a suggestion for a new tool? We'd love to hear it.
